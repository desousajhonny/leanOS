# MVP Launch

## Purpose

Launch the MVP into a focused learning loop.

## Inputs

- Product positioning
- Landing page copy
- Acquisition channels
- Launch goal
- Customer feedback plan

## Process

1. Read Marketing AGENT and choose Growth Lead
2. Use `skills/define-positioning.skill.md` if positioning is unclear
3. Use `skills/create-landing-page-copy.skill.md` to prepare launch copy
4. Use `skills/create-launch-plan.skill.md` to choose channels and learning metrics
5. Route visual design to Operations Design when needed
6. Route budget/pricing implications to Growth Finance when needed
7. Plan how Customer Experience will capture feedback

## Output

- Launch plan
- Landing page copy
- Acquisition experiment
- Learning loop
- Open risks

## Files to Update

- Update `../knowledge/positioning.md`, `../knowledge/landing-page.md`, `../knowledge/acquisition-channels.md` or `../knowledge/launch-plan.md` after explicit confirmation.

## Navigation

Start from `../AGENT.md`, choose a role in `../roles/`, load required skills in `../skills/`, then use this playbook.
