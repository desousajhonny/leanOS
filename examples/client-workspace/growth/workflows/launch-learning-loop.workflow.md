# Launch Learning Loop Workflow

## Purpose

Coordinate marketing, customer experience and finance after launch.

## Required Areas

- marketing
- customer-experience

## Availability

All required areas are active in this department.


## Sequence

1. Read Marketing AGENT and launch knowledge
2. Plan launch
3. Read Customer Experience AGENT and capture customer feedback
4. Review Finance AGENT when pricing, budget or unit economics are involved
5. Recommend next learning loop
