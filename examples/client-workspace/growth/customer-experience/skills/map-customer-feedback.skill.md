# Map Customer Feedback

## Purpose

Cluster feedback into product, support and growth signals.

## Use When

- new customer feedback arrives
- feedback needs to inform roadmap or growth
- signals need clustering

## Required Context

- Customer feedback knowledge
- Product context when available
- Current roadmap when available

## Inputs

- Feedback
- Customer segment
- Source
- Frequency
- Impact

## Process

1. Remove private details
2. Cluster by problem or desired outcome
3. Separate signal from opinion
4. Identify product/growth/support impact
5. Recommend next owner

## Checks

- No private customer data is stored
- Patterns are not overclaimed
- Roadmap impact is routed to Strategy/Roadmap

## Output

- Feedback clusters
- Learning themes
- Recommended next owner

## Files to Update

- Update `../knowledge/customer-feedback.md` after explicit confirmation.

## Red Lines

- Do not invent evidence.
- Do not treat one-off feedback as validation.
