# New Idea Intake Workflow

## Purpose

Capture, qualify and decide the next destination of a founder idea before it becomes roadmap, MVP scope or implementation work.

## Required Areas

- product
- roadmap

## Availability

All required areas are active in this department.


## Sequence

1. Read product strategy
2. Restate the founder idea in plain language
3. Evaluate idea against ICP, problem, value proposition, evidence and current focus
4. Identify assumptions, evidence gaps and opportunity cost
5. Route to Roadmap only if backlog or sequencing impact needs review
6. Recommend reject, park, validation note, backlog candidate or roadmap candidate
7. Ask for confirmation before recording the idea anywhere

## Navigation

Use Strategy area READMEs for each step to preserve area-first ownership.
