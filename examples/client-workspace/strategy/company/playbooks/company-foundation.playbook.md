# Company Foundation

## Purpose

Move from raw company context to usable operating principles.

## Area

`strategy/company`

## Sequence

1. Read company profile
2. Clarify mission
3. List principles
4. Define operating model
5. Record decisions

## Outputs

- Decision or action summary
- Updated source-of-truth files when requested
- Next recommended LeanOS command
