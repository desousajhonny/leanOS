# MVP Validation

## Purpose

Run the validation loop from assumption to roadmap impact.

## Inputs

- Product strategy
- MVP scope
- Assumption register
- Riskiest assumptions
- Experiment plan
- Success metrics

## Process

1. Identify the assumption being tested
2. Classify the risk and why it matters
3. Design the smallest experiment that can produce evidence
4. Define success and failure signals before running the experiment
5. Collect evidence without interpreting it as fact too early
6. Separate evidence from insight
7. Make or defer a decision
8. Update roadmap or backlog only when the decision requires it

## Output

- Validated learning summary
- Evidence vs insight separation
- Decision or explicit no-decision
- Roadmap or backlog impact
- Next validation action

## Files to Update

- Update `../assumptions.md` when assumptions are added or reclassified.
- Update `../riskiest-assumptions.md` when priority changes.
- Update `../experiments.md` when an experiment is planned or completed.
- Update `../success-metrics.md` when signals are defined or changed.
- Update `../learning-log.md` only when evidence supports learning.
- Propose changes to `../../roadmap/knowledge/roadmap.md` or `../../roadmap/knowledge/backlog.md` only after a decision is confirmed.
