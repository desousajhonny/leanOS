# Define ICP

## Purpose

Define the first customer segment with pains, triggers and exclusions.

## Use When

- the target customer is too broad
- validation needs a first segment
- marketing or design needs a clearer audience

## Required Context

- ../knowledge/icp.md
- ../knowledge/problem.md
- ../../validation/assumptions.md

## Inputs

- Primary user
- Buyer or decision maker
- Pain trigger
- Exclusion criteria
- Evidence level

## Process

1. Choose the narrowest useful first segment.
2. Clarify buyer, user and decision maker.
3. List qualification criteria and exclusions.
4. Connect ICP to assumptions that need validation.

## Checks

- ICP is specific enough to recruit or interview.
- Exclusions are listed.
- Assumptions are not presented as validated facts.

## Output

- ICP draft
- Recruiting or validation criteria
- Assumptions to validate

## Files to Update

- ../knowledge/icp.md
- ../../validation/assumptions.md

## Red Lines

- Do not define everyone as the ICP.
- Do not skip exclusions.
- Do not create Growth acquisition plans from ICP alone.
